Power BI: Survey Data Visualizations

  • Led a comprehensive data analysis project utilizing Power BI to extract actionable insights from a survey dataset on data professions, encompassing demographics, job roles, salary ranges, and job satisfaction metrics.
  • Cleaned data with custom delimiting and string manipulation, to refine and standardize responses in columns related to job titles and programming languages, improving data accuracy.
  • Applied statistical methods to compute the average salary range from provided intervals, enabling a more precise assessment of income trends among survey respondents.
  • Designed an intuitive dashboard featuring diverse visualizations, including stacked bar and column charts, treemaps, gauges, and donut charts, offering a comprehensive overview of survey results.
  • Created a clear and effective layout with color scheme adjustments to enhance dashboard aesthetics, ensuring information is presented in an easily digestible manner for readers.

Tableau: Seatle Air BnB 2016 Data Analysis

  • Analyzed a dataset of over 23 million records from Seattle Airbnb listings spanning 2016 and 2017.
  • Curated data by performing inner joins between Listings, Calendar, and Reviews tables, ensuring seamless integration for meaningful insights.
  • Created interactive visualizations, such as "Price by Zipcode" and "Price Per Zipcode," to present average pricing trends across different Seattle neighborhoods.
  • Created a "Revenue for Year" line chart, enabling precise identification of high-revenue periods and offering valuable insights for business planning..
  • Produced "Distinct Count of Bedrooms Listings" visualization, offering a clear picture of competition levels based on bedroom counts in the Airbnb market.

Tableau: Covid Data Visualizations

  • Transformed raw covid-19 data into insightful visualizations, including 'global numbers' text table, 'total deaths per continent' bar chart, 'population infected per country' map, and 'percent population infected over time' line chart.
  • Utilized tableau's geospatial capabilities to map covid-19 infection rates by country, enhancing data representation and facilitating geographic insights.
  • Employed forecasting techniques to predict future infection trends.
  • Created a user-friendly dashboard, combining multiple visualizations and legends to present complex data concisely, enabling readers to quickly grasp critical information.
